Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, fondly known as the "Missile Man of India", was a visionary scientist and the 11th President of India. He inspired millions through his humility, wisdom, and dedication to education and nation-building.

Why I Admire Him

  • He was a brilliant aerospace scientist who played a pivotal role in India's missile and space programs.
  • He remained humble and accessible, even as President of India.
  • He dedicated his life to inspiring students and encouraging innovation.
